How to use two Whatsapp accounts on the same phone (Huawei)

Thinking of how to use two Whatsapp accounts on the same phone? Then what are you waiting for, read this How-To Blog to find out how.

Dual SIM is great, but dual messaging is even better.

Using two Whatsapp accounts on the same phone comes in very handy for people who don't use two seperate phones for work and for personel tasks.

Several manufacturers have natively implemented a solution to let people simultaneously use two accounts with the same messaging app. The compatibility will depend on the phone's brand, model, and software version. If your device supports it, it's by far the easiest and most convenient way to text with two different WhatsApp numbers on your phone. With this method, notifications will be handled natively, and most phones will identify the secondary account with a badge.

Samsung

If you have a Samsung phone with One UI, you've probably noticed your phone asked if you wanted to use a secondary WhatsApp account when installing the app. If you dismissed the prompt then, have no fear, as it's effortless to set up a second copy of the app.

  • Go to Settings
  • Navigate to Advanced features
  • Click on Dual Messenger
  • Select Whatsapp
